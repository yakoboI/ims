Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Inventory Management System ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our service.

Data We Collect

Personal Information

  • Username and email address
  • Full name and contact information
  • Authentication credentials (encrypted)

Usage Data

  • Login timestamps and session information
  • User actions and system interactions
  • Device and browser information

Business Data

  • Inventory items and stock levels
  • Sales and purchase transactions
  • Customer and supplier information
  • Financial records and reports

Cookie Usage

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Necessary Cookies: Required for authentication and system functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Store your settings (theme, language, etc.)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage patterns (optional)

You can manage cookie preferences through the cookie settings button on any page.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To authenticate users and manage access
  • To process transactions and manage inventory
  • To generate reports and analytics
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted authentication tokens
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and user permissions

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request data deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent for optional cookies

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us through the system settings or your system administrator.
